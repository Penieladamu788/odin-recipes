Baked Tomato Slices

Baked tomato slices are a delicious way to top leftover pasta.

Preparation Time

Prep Time:

10 mins

Cook Time:

5 mins

Total Time:

15 mins

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided or as needed
  • 1 large tomato, cut into 1/2-inch-thick slice
  • i spring fresh rosemary, leaves stripped and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Brush a baking sheet with about 1 tablespoon of olive oil.

Step 2

Arrange tomato slices on the baking sheet; sprinkle rosemary and garlic on top.
Brush with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil; season with salt and pepper.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
