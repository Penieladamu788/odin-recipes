Prosciutto e Melone (Italian Ham and Melon)

Prosciutto and melon are paired to make a super easy, 2-ingredient, refreshing appetizer or snack:
just the cantaloupe into bite-sized process, wrap them with ham and spear them on skewers. Use superior-quality Italian ham for best results.

Preparation Time

Prep Time:

15 mins

Total Time:

10 mins

Servings

4

Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe-seeded and cut into 8 wedges
  • 8 this slices prosciutto

Directions

Step 3

Remove the flask from the rind of the cantalogue; wrap each piece of the cantaloupe
with a slice of prosciutto. Arrange on a platter and serve cold.
